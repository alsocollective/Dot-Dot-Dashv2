About Us

DOT DOT DASH is an independent marketing firm specializing in the development of handcrafted content and communications. Our practice leans towards the digital and social landscapes, but extends into fully integrated services. With scalable production capacity, we focus on busine.sses and organizations that stand for something and who want to use communication for good. We only work with great and virtuous organizations to ensure that our clients are always in good company. We inspire action through love, story, conversation and the building of communities. We believe more in long-term commitments to communications with customers and less in ‘one off’ campaigns. OUr goal is to foster a series of strategic conversations that keep our clients’ brands top of mind. We value sustainable growth over instant solutions. We believe in using technology to help brands build cultural relevance. We partner with visionary clients to build strategies that instigate dialogue and experience. We create work that people want to know about and be a part of. We create things that are optimistic, inclusive, valuable and relevant. We’re as tired of today’s over-saturated media landscape and inauthentic brand messaging as you are. Like many, we got tired of being sold crap that we didn’t need or want. So we decided to do something about it by creating a new business hardwired to work in a new economy with a new consumner. We encourage our clients to be honest, progressive and transparent and help find ways for the market to reward them for being this way. We also encourage our clients to be smart, strategic and original. We do this because we believe in these values ourselves and we believe the consumer deserves better than what they are getting today. We care about nature, connnunity and culture and we care about integrity and imagination.

Our Process

We don’t have any formal training in mark·etinq and it shows. We are nondoqmatic and generally don’t care about existing rules or protocols. We blur the lines between account and creative personnel and encourage our teams to get completely immersed in solving business problems. We speak to our clients in normal language rather than jargon. We treat our clients, and their customers, as humans, not as metrics or demogiaphics. What we do care about is success. We want to keep our campaigns and our jobs interesting and imaginative. We did not set out to compete on level ground with other advertising agencies, so we use asymmetrical tactics to get results, often big one’s. We believe in ourselves and we believe in our clients. It shows in our work. If you are a great organization or cornpany, and want to do something oriqinal, please give us a call. If you want to roll out another same-old seasonal campaign around a mundane and tired product, differentiated from its competition only by branding, p1ease call someone else.
